Born in the heart of Bali, Indonesia, in 2022, 96 is the brainchild of Miguel Rengifo, a Colombian designer and entrepreneur with a passion for innovation. Our studio was built on the belief that great design can transform ideas into impactful products. Over the years, weve partnered with startups to help them navigate the journey from zero to one, focusing on time to value and user experience. Today, many of our clients have grown into successful ventures, raising millions and making their mark. At 96, were proud to bring a global perspective to every project we touch.
